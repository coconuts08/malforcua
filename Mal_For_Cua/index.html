Gender Equality Awareness Campaign

Key Facts

Gender-based discrimination affects millions globally.

Every year, millions of girls are denied education.

Women still earn less than men in many professions.

Take Action

Goals of Gender Equality

End all forms of discrimination and violence against women and girls.

Eliminate harmful practices such as child marriage and female genital mutilation.

Ensure women's full and effective participation and equal opportunities for leadership at all levels of decision-making in political, economic, and public life.

Ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health and rights, including family planning services.

Undertake reforms to give women equal rights to economic resources, access to ownership and control over land and other forms of property, financial services, inheritance, and natural resources.

Enhance the use of enabling technology, in particular information and communications technology, to promote the empowerment of women.

Adopt and strengthen policies and enforceable legislation for the promotion of gender equality and the empowerment of all women and girls at all levels.
